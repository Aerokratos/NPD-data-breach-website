2023

The Initial Security Breach

The security incident at National Public Data (NPD) occurred in December 2023 but remained undetected for months. The breach exposed personal data of approximately 272 million individuals.

The database circulated in underground criminal markets, setting the stage for one of the most significant data exposure incidents in recent history.

2024

USDoD Offers Data for Sale

A hacker known as “USDoD” publicly advertised a massive dataset allegedly stolen from National Public Data on underground forums.
